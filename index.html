Amazing Animal Sanctuaries

Our Sanctuaries have incredibly unique programs that rescue animals from captive situations and rehabilitate them so they can live and roam freely within large natural habitats with others of their own kind.

However, the Sanctuary is not a Zoo and offers a completely different experience than what most people are used to. Our rescued animals live in large acreage open habitats and are never forced to remain front-and-center just so people can see them easier.

We currently care for more than 600 Lions, Tigers, Bears, Wolves and other rescued animals! The Wild Animal Sanctuary is a non-profit organization and does not receive government support or special funding. 100% of the support that we receive is given by someone like you who cares.
